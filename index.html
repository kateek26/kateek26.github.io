About The Rookie

The Rookie is about John Nolan, an older man who becomes a new police officer in Los Angeles. He trains with experienced cops like Sergeant Grey and his strict training officer Talia Bishop, while learning how to handle real police work. Other rookies, like Lucy Chen and Jackson West, are also figuring out the job and face their own challenges. Together, they deal with crimes, danger, and personal struggles. The show focuses on teamwork, growth, and proving themselves as officers.
